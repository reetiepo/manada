Letter to Enthusiasts

Hello, enthusiast. We from Manada, just like you, believe in blockchain. We can see its benefits and inevitability in a world about to come, however, by now, we can only have a glimpse narrowed by the lens of human imagination.

We know that blockchain is real, but this emergent state does not allow a prosper future guaranteed. We know how much can be built with its structure, however, this inevitability will only be an illusion if we do not start the application of its concepts in a way to entangle people lives by solving problems in a creative way.

For this, the blockchain shouldn’t be faced purely under the financial speculation, especially if only made to empower the pyramid top. The decentralization must start its impact in society in a way that can make people believe and value it transformation power.

This is what Manada wants to build. Today, we are approaching a minimum fraction of the blockchain potential, however, in a way to help it grow, we strive to utilize the current blockchain ecosystem in all our processes and we are really thankful for the tools and knowledge made by all these other enthusiasts to help us show the positives impacts of this technology either. ♥ getmonero.org, coinhive.com, cryptonote.org, electrum.org, bitcoin.org

We don’t care if you believe that bitcoin is the future, or litecoin, or b-cash (sorry Roger), or any other crypto. We care for the fact that you believe in blockchain as the revolution that it is and believe in it as instrument for making our society better.

We are living a new relationship of value generation, and we are under the shadow of indeterminacy that will drive the future. The technique by itself will not be responsible for this new time, but the quality of human relationship will, so it’s up to us, enthusiasts, to apply the blockchain potential in the widest range possible for making things happen.

If you believe this, you are part of it.

Let’s build the way!

Thank you very much for reading this manifesto. Learn more about Manada and feel free to collaborate.
